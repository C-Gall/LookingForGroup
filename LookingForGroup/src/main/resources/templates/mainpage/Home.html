Welcome to LookingForGroup!

LookingForGroup is a social network that aims to bring veterans and newcomers to the world of TTRPGs together.
Although TTRPGs can be fun, it's better when you can find the group you mesh with. Maybe you prefer serious
campaigns with dark and gritty storytelling, or maybe you want a lighthearted and humorous experience. Perhaps
you're having trouble finding a game for a system that is not quite popular yet. LookingForGroup aims to help
players like you find groups either online or in person that fit your playstyle.

In addition to finding games, LookingForGroup offers a social network experience, letting you become friends
with players you've come across, *create posts and comments, *chat with other users, *and join special interest
groups within our social network.

*If you are a content creator, you are also able to post and share your third party content with the community, both
for advertising and feedback!

So what are you waiting for, and join LookingForGroup today!
